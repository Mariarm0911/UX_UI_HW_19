About me

From Blueprints to Pixels: Architectural Expertise in UX/UI Design.

Hello! my name is María and I am a Designer, Food Recipe Creator, Triathlete, Flow Arts enthusiast, and Founder of the non-profit initiative "Steps for Equity" which supports women in sports in Mexico. After earning my degree in architecture in Mexico in October 2016, I dove into the commercial industry as a full-time Architectural Designer. I am expanding my knowledge and skills to User Experience Design at UC Berkeley Boot camp. I'm transitioning from architectural design to UX/UI design with enthusiasm, currently seeking the right internship. My background in architecture brings significant value to UX/UI design. I live in San Francisco, CA, and work at HED (an Architectural Firm) on Healthcare, Science & Technology, and K-12 projects. Before moving to the Bay Area, I worked at "Cinepolis" (a Mexican Worldwide Cinema Company) as an Architectural Designer /Space Planner in 2016. Earlier that year, I graduated with honors from UMSNH in Morelia Michoacán Mexico, where I obtained my undergraduate degree in Architecture. I consider myself a curious person who loves to learn new things, visit new places, and engage in outdoor activities. One of the things I love about living in SF is being part of a diverse community, which enhances my self-awareness and provides insights into my own thought processes, life experiences, and values. In December 2022, I organized a 5k and 10k race for @stepsforequity, a non-profit initiative that I founded to support women in sports in a small community in Michoacán, Mexico. The event was designed to create a less intimidating environment and generate social awareness for future events, welcoming women of all fitness levels and ages. I organized a fundraiser to cover the basic expenses to make it free and open enrollment for all women from the community. Apart from my professional and non-profit work, I am passionate about flow arts, an activity that combines the mind and body to connect with my artistic self. When I am not busy building or designing, you can find me training for the next triathlon, creating delicious recipes at @apron4us, or dancing.
